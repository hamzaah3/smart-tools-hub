## Best Practices

When converting PDFs to images, keep these tips in mind:

- **Choose the Right Format**: 
  - JPG for photos and complex graphics
  - PNG for documents with text and transparency
  - WEBP for modern web applications

- **Optimize Quality**: Balance between file size and image quality based on your needs

- **Check Resolution**: Ensure the output resolution meets your requirements (300 DPI for print, 72-96 DPI for web)
